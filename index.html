Fractal Mainnet Bootstrap Program

Fractal’s inaugural Community Rewards Program on mainnet launch.

Introducing: Fractal Mainnet Bootstrap Program

At Fractal, we believe in building a thriving ecosystem that is driven by its community. To celebrate the launch of the Fractal Mainnet and recognize active participants on Bitcoin, we are excited to announce the Fractal Mainnet Bootstrap Program.

In this program, a total of 1 million Fractal (FB)—Fractal's native asset—will be distributed among eligible users.

This initiative is a community-focused rewards program designed to reward the most engaged and dedicated Bitcoin users with FB, Fractal's native asset.

We envision a future where Fractal is a hub for innovation, creativity, and decentralized growth. The Fractal Mainnet Bootstrap Program is just the beginning of a series of community-centric initiatives we plan to roll out.

Users can check their eligibility for this exciting opportunity by tapping the links on this page.

Together, let's build a more robust and vibrant Fractal ecosystem!
